Biography

Hashim Abu-gellban currently works at Texas Tech University (TTU). He received his Ph.D. degree from Texas Tech University. He has two M.S. degree from New Mexico State University, and the University of Jordan. He received his a B.S. degree from Yarmouk University.

Dr. Abu-gellban's general research interests are Time Series Classification, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, and Bioinformatics. He has published prolifically in refereed journals and conference proceedings, such as IEEE Annual Computer Software and Applications Conference (COMPSAC), ACM International Symposium on Spatial and Temporal Databases, ACM Workshop on Information Hiding and Multimedia Security, IEEE International Conference on Big Data (Big Data), IEEE International Conference on Intelligence and Security Informatics (ISI), IEEE International Conference on Computational Science and Computational Intelligence (CSCI), ACM Symposium on Signal Processing Systems (SSPS), and Springer International Conference on Data Science (ICDATA).

Dr. Abu-gellban is a member of several programme committees: IEEE BigData 2022 - Applications of Big Data Technology in the Transport Industry, IEEE BigData 2022 - 5th Workshop on Big Data for CyberSecurity "BigCyber-2022", IEEE BigData 2022 - Workshop on Big Data Analytic in Healthcare, and IEEE DSAA 2022 - 9th IEEE International Conference on Data Science and Advanced Analytics . Moreover, he serves as a reviewer for PLOS Digital Health, and PLOS ONE journals.
